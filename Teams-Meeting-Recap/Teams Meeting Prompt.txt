Generate a meeting recap from the transcript. Follow the structure exactly. Be concise, specific, and evidence based. Do not invent details.

CITATIONS (ONLY WHEN YOU INCLUDE SUBSTANTIVE CONTENT)
- If you state an Outcome, Decision, Action Item, Risk/Blocker, Open Question, Dependency, Deferred Topic, or Next Checkpoint detail, include at least one citation in this format: (Speaker, HH:MM:SS).
- Use at most 2 citations per bullet.
- Do NOT add citations for "None captured" lines or for "Unknown" reasons.

SILENCE RULE (CRITICAL FOR READABILITY)
- If a section has no content, output ONLY:
  - None captured.
- Do not explain why.
- Do not mention scanning.
- Do not include timestamps.

UNKNOWN RULE (MINIMAL, NO NOISE)
- If a field is Unknown, include a short reason phrase only.
  - Example: "Owner: Unknown. Reason: not assigned in transcript."
- Do not include timestamps for Unknown reasons.

QUALITY RULES
- Outcomes are not a list of topics. Only include items that materially changed understanding, direction, risk, or next steps.
- Each Executive Outcome must include a "Why selected" line.
- Only call something a Decision if a choice was explicitly confirmed (approved, agreed, decided, "we will do X").
- Action items must have: Owner, Due (or TBD), Done means, and Dependencies (or None).
